Frequently Asked Questions

How long is the service?

Roughly 2 hours, but it depends on the assignments.

How often should I detail my car?

Once every 4 to 6 months.

How do I book an appointment?

There are 3 ways to book an appointment:
- Come into our office and physically make an appointment.
- Contact our number: (123) 456-7890.
- Contact our email: pvdetail@pvamu.edu.

What specific detail jobs do you work?

We do a variety of works such as: window tint, respray, tire polish and shine, car wash, full interior cleaning, headlight restoration and more.

What are your price ranges

The price is determined by what specific assignments you give us to do.

What is the difference between a basic and a full detail?

That is determined by the customer. Originally, a basic detail includes a wash, wax and vaccum, but a full detail has a few more elements added, which is based on what the customer wants us to work on.

Do we need to wash or clean out our cars before detailing?

We advise you to remove any items from your vehicle before we conduct intertior detailing. For any exterior detailing, we prefer you to hand wash your car beforehand.
