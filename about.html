Message from Our Team

Pvamu Auto Detailers was founded February 2022, It’s a small owned business that comprises students with backgrounds on cars and who understand the need for a car to be taken care of properly. We understand the frustration of having to manage school, work life , social life and also making sure your car is clean and maintained. That’s why we are here for you. Pvamu Car details believes that there's no vehicle that is "too dirty " and we aim to prove it. Honesty and transparency are pillars to our success, we treat every customer as a friend. Our team members are strong believers that customers satisfaction should be a business top priority.
